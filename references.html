References

Transport Accident Commission. (n.d.). Road Safety Quarterly Statistics.
https://www.tac.vic.gov.au/road-safety/statistics/road-safety-statistical-summary
Description of data: The data is quantitative . Data outlines the quarterly reports of Road accidents of the last 12 months from June 2025 and the last 12 months from December 2024. The reason why there is overlap in the dates the data is measured in, is so the transport accident commission can track data continuously without needing to wait a full year. Quarterly reports of the previous years are also included. The data will be used to outline general trends in road safety in Victoria of the last quarterly reports.

Victorian Government. (2025, August 11).Victorian Road trauma statistics.
https://www.vic.gov.au/victorian-road-trauma-statistics
Description of data: The data is quantitative. An annual report of the road accidents that have occurred in 2024, including data of the number of lives lost from road accidents, number of people with serious injury and types of injuries occurring in these road accidents. The 2023 annual report is also included. The 2025 annual report has not yet been uploaded. The data will be used to outline general trends in road safety in Victoria in 2025.

Transport Victoria. (2026). Victoria road crash data
https://opendata.transport.vic.gov.au/dataset/victoria-road-crash-data
Description of data: The data is categorical and quantitative. A report that covers data relating to road incidents in 2024. The data ranges from information about which cities of Victoria have the most road accidents to whether or not police were attending during an accident. The data is extensive in including all relevant variables in road safety. 2025 is yet to be updated as of investigating our topic. The data will be used to outline the general trends in road safety in the City of Monash.

City of Monash. (2021). Road management plan.
https://www.monash.vic.gov.au/About-Us/Corporate-Strategies-Plans/Plans-and-Strategies/Road-Management-Plan
Description of data: The data is qualitative.The data outlines the road management plan the City of Monash has towards improving road safety. The plan was created in 2021 and will be used until 2030. It has also been revised several times. The data will be used to outline a timeline of the City of Monash’s plans for road safety.

City of Monash.(2025). Annual Report
https://www.monash.vic.gov.au/About-Us/Our-Organisation/Our-Performance/Annual-Report
Description of data: The data is qualitative.The data provides an annual report of the City of Monash’s accomplishments during 2025, including their achievements in road safety . There are also the annual reports of the previous years. The data will be used to compare how the City of Monash managed to implement their regulations in reference to their plans.
