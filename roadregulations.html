Which speed zones in the City of Monash have the highest number of accidents?

Analysis

This graph shows that the most number of accidents in the City of Monash occur in the 60 and 80 km/h zones being 1804 and 1354 respectively, while the 40 km/h speed zones have the least number of accidents, with it being 174. This may be because there is a high amount of speed zones that are 80km/h or 60km/h or that people are less aware while driving in those speed zones.

Conclusion

While the City of Monash’s lack of road regulation can add to the reason why more abundant speed zones like 60km/h and 80km/h have the most number of accidents. It can be attributed to awareness of drivers in this area and not directly the City of Monash’s fault.
