Which roads in the City of Monash are the most dangerous?

This website presents an analysis of road crash locations within the City of Monash. The data has been visualised using Google My Maps and Google Sheets.

Analysis

The road with the highest number of accidents is Monash Freeway. As a high-speed, high-density freeway, it contributes to loss-of-control accidents, particularly during poor weather conditions.

Major state routes such as Dandenong Road (Princes Highway), Wellington Road, Springvale Road and Warrigal Road are moderate-speed, high-density roads with varying road conditions. Factors such as road maintenance, lane width, markings, and pedestrian/cyclist separation contribute to accident frequency.

Conclusion

Overall, roads with higher traffic density and usage tend to have a higher number of accidents. This suggests that increased safety measures and improved infrastructure may be necessary on the City of Monash’s most heavily used roads.
